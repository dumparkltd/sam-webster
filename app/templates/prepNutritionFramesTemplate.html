Sam's Nutrition

Nutrition is crucial for building stamina and strength, and to recover well from training.

Select training phase

  • Aerobic Fitness
  • High Intensity
  • Tapering

Aerobic Fitness
Slower average speeds at 50-60 percent of maximum, and longer road rides up to and around 60 minutes a day. A generally moderate energy intake. Carbohydrate intake is controlled. Quality protein sources are important, including after training.

High intensity
Speeds of over 60kph on the track, with five or six intense efforts per training session. Carbohydrates are increased to match requirements, as are proteins to enhance recovery and adaptation. Crucial focus on regular timings during the day. Increased fluid intake.
